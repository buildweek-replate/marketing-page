You have a surplus of food at the end of the day. What do you do with it, throw it away? We have a solution to reduce your food waste, and help others at the same time.

A Replate volunteer will come to you, and pick up the surplus food that you wish to donate. We will then provide it to one of our worthy partner organizations who will take it to those that need it most!
