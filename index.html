♥ You have a surplus of food at the end of the day. What do you do with it, throw it away?

♥ 40% of food in the US is wasted, while 1 in 7 people are food insecure!

♥ We have a solution to reduce your food waste, and help others at the same time.



♥ A Replate volunteer will come to you, and pick up the surplus food that you wish to donate.

♥ We will then provide it to one of our worthy partner organizations who will take it to those that need it most!

♥ You can help bridge this disconnect in our society by donating food, or volunteering your time.
